Roast pork leg

Mouthwatering and succulent roasted pork leg that just melts in your mouth, it's so tender.

Ingredients
  • 2 Â½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons dried sage
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1(5 pound) pork leg with skin
  • 1 large onion
  • 6 cloves, halved

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 430 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Combine olive oil, sage, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Rinse pork leg and pat dry with a paper towel. Rub seasoned olive oil onto the pork leg.
  4. Slice onion and separate layers into rings. Place onion rings and garlic halves in the bottom of a roaster. Place pork on top of onions and garlic.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven for 40 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 355 degrees F (180 degrees C) and roast until an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center reads at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C), about 1 hour 20 minutes more.
  6. Remove from the oven and slice and serve.
