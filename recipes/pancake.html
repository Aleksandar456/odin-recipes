Uncle's pancakes

Learn a skill for life with Uncle's foolproof easy crêpe recipe that ensures perfect pancakes every time - elaborate flip optional

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

  • ½ teaspoon salt

  • 4 large egg whites

  • 3 cups milk

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  • 1 tablespoon butter for the pan, or more as needed

Directions

  1. Sift together flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.

  2. Beat egg whites in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl until stiff peaks form. Set aside.

  3. Mix milk, egg yolks, oil, and vanilla extract in a large bowl. Whisk in flour mixture until well combined. Fold in egg whites with a spatula.

  4. Heat a small piece of butter in a skillet and pour a small amount of batter into the skillet. Turn skillet so batter covers the bottom of the skillet.

  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip pancake over and cook on the other side for 1 to 2 minutes more.

  6. Repeat with remaining batter, always melting a new small piece of butter in the skillet first.

  7. Serve with jam or cheese for a sweet or salty experience and enjoy with some tea. :))
